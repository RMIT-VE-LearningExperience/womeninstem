      Safety Briefing

      Make The Site Safe

      You are the safety officer on a building under construction. Walk every level, climb the ladders, and secure all hazards before the crew starts work.

      • Find all 5 hazards across the ground, upper floors, and roof deck.
      • Climb ladders with Up / Down to move between levels.
      • Standing too close to an active hazard raises the risk meter.
      • If risk hits 100% or time runs out, the round fails.

      How to Play

      Your Goal: Inspect the construction site, find all 5 OH&S hazards, and secure them before the crew starts work.

      Move through every level of the building, watch the risk meter, and make the site safe before time runs out.

      Controls:

      • Left / Right: Walk along the current level
      • Up / Down: Climb ladders between levels when you are nearby
      • Spacebar / Enter / Secure: Secure a hazard when you are standing close to it
      • P / Pause: Pause or resume the shift

      Tips:

      1. Start on the ground level and work your way up through the ladders
      2. Use the checklist to track which hazards are still active
      3. When the status bar names a hazard, press Space or tap Secure to secure it
      4. Keep moving so you can clear every zone before the timer reaches zero

      Watch Out:

      Standing too close to an active hazard raises the risk meter. If risk reaches 100%, the inspection fails.

      Secure all hazards before time expires to complete the shift safely.
