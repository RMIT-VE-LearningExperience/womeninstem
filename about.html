About Build Your Path

An interactive way to discover construction pathways, skills, and real opportunities.

This experience is built to help students, educators, and career changers understand what construction roles involve and where they can lead.

Innovation begins with curiosity, and success is built through courage and action.

What Is Build Your Path?

Build Your Path is an interactive platform designed to introduce people to the diverse range of careers available in the construction industry. Through immersive mini games, career exploration tools, and engaging content, it aims to inspire the next generation of builders.

Our Mission

Construction offers rewarding, well-paid careers that are often overlooked. The mission is to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and show that there is a place for everyone in the construction industry.

What You Can Do Here

  • Explore different construction career pathways
  • Play mini games that simulate real construction tasks
  • Learn about career progression from entry-level to senior roles
  • Discover what skills and qualifications you need
