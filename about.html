About Build Your Path

Discover construction pathways, skills, and real-world careers of the future.

Build Your Path is an interactive online experience for high school students, educators, key influencers, and career changers.

Developed by RMIT's Women in STEM and Digital Design teams, the platform helps people understand what different construction roles involve, the skills they use, and where those pathways can lead.

What it is

A digital and VR pathway into construction

Build Your Path introduces people to the varied careers available in construction and supports greater gender diversity in the sector.

From hands-on trades and engineering to project management, finance, data and digital roles, human resources, legal, surveying and more, it highlights how many different skills and interests can lead to a rewarding construction career.

Image placeholder VR experience in action

How it works

Explore, play, and hear real stories

Through immersive mini games, career exploration tools, and stories from real professionals, participants can explore what it is like to work in construction.

In RMIT-facilitated workshops, players wear VR headsets and step into a construction environment where they speak with workers, learn how teams are organised, and try virtual roles such as crane operator and project manager.

What you can do here

  • Explore different pathways into the construction industry
  • Complete a quiz to find roles that might suit you
  • Play mini games that simulate real construction tasks
  • Learn about progression from entry-level to senior roles
  • Discover the skills, qualifications, and attributes that help you get started

Why it matters

There is a place for everyone in construction

Construction offers rewarding, well-paid, and meaningful careers, yet many people do not see themselves in the industry.

Women and gender-diverse people remain significantly underrepresented, making up only around 12-14% of the workforce and about 2-3% of trade and site-based roles. Build Your Path helps challenge stereotypes and give students and career seekers the information and experiences needed to explore construction pathways.

Women in STEM at RMIT

Practical, inspiring STEM pathways

The Women in STEM team at RMIT is a group of educators, industry practitioners, and outreach specialists who work to open STEM futures for underrepresented groups, especially women and gender-diverse people.

Build Your Path was inspired by Nailed It!, an RMIT program that gave students hands-on exposure to trades including carpentry, plumbing, and electrical.

Digital Design team at RMIT

Interactive stories, games, and VR

RMIT's CoVE Digital Media Design team led the development of the immersive digital and virtual reality experience, bringing together visual design, video production, interactive media, 3D development, and learning design.

The project brings industry stories and career pathways to life through hands-on activities that help participants see themselves in construction roles.

Contributors

Made possible through industry and university collaboration

Build Your Path has been made possible through funding from the M80 Ring Road Project as part of Victoria's Big Build and RMIT University. The project is led by the RMIT Women in STEM team and recognises the valuable contributions from M80 Ring Road staff who shared their stories and career pathways.
